Tema: Isolation / Premissa: Keyboard input only


Daniel
Your boat is wrecked on an unknown shore. To leave, you must find three materials scattered across a dark, ominous forest: Wood, a Sail, and Rope. But you are not alone. A voice watches your every move, mocking your past and questioning your choices.

There is only one rule written in the sand: Obey him.

FEATURES

Use your light to navigate
Uncover Daniel's troubled past
A narrative-driven experience focused on tension.
3 TRIALS

The Garden
The Maze
The Burden
CONTROLS

WASD/Arrows: Move
E: Toggle Flashlight
SPACE: Interact / Pick up items
